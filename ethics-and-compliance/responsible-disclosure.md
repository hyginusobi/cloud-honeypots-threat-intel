# Responsible Disclosure

## Purpose
This project follows responsible disclosure principles to ensure that findings improve security without enabling misuse.

---

## Disclosure Approach
- Findings presented at a high level
- No publication of exploitable configurations
- No exposure of live credentials or secrets
- Indicators shared for defensive awareness only

---

## Audience
- Security professionals
- Academic reviewers
- Hiring organisations
- Defensive research community

---

## Outcome
The project contributes to improved defensive understanding while avoiding operational risk.
