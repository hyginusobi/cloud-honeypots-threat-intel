# Legal Considerations

## Scope of Activity
All activity within this project remained within the legal boundaries of defensive cybersecurity research.

- No unauthorised access to external systems
- No interference with attacker infrastructure
- No breach of cloud provider acceptable use policies

---

## Jurisdictional Awareness
The honeypots were deployed in cloud environments subject to provider terms and applicable regional regulations.

Key considerations included:
- Cloud service acceptable use policies
- Data protection regulations
- Cybercrime legislation

---

## Compliance Measures
- Passive data collection only
- Sanitised storage of logs
- No attribution attempts
- No data sharing outside defensive research context

---

## Legal Conclusion
The project complies with legal expectations for ethical security research and defensive monitoring.
