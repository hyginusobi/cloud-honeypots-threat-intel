# Triage Process

## Purpose
To rapidly assess and prioritise detected events.

---

## Triage Steps
1. Confirm alert source
2. Assess severity
3. Identify attack phase
4. Correlate related events

---

## Severity Indicators
- Repeated authentication attempts
- Discovery command execution
- Privilege escalation attempts

---

## Output
- Event categorisation
- Risk scoring
- Escalation decision
